  • MAIN DISHES & SALAD
    •
    1. Nyama Choma - Grilled meat, typically goat or beef, served with kachumbari (salsa).
    2. Ugali - A staple maize porridge, often served with meat or vegetable stews.
    3. Pilau - Spiced rice dish, often made with chicken or beef.
    4. Fish Fry - Fried fresh fish, typically tilapia, served with a lemon or tartar sauce.
    5. Vegetable Stew - A mix of seasonal vegetables cooked in a rich sauce.
    SALAD
  • Kachumbari - Fresh tomato and onion salad, often with chili.
